Description

- Left click on the screen to move the blue circle.

- Shoot the enemies, Aim on them and press Q.

- Each shot has 1 second cooldown.

- Enemies will spawn every 1 second, and they dont despawn.

- Careful though, every 8 seconds a different type of moving enemy spawns. It's much faster than the enemy that you're used to.

- Shooting enemies gives +50 exp and +20 level exp. And the miniboss gives +100 exp and +80 level exp

- Every time you level up, your frenzy count goes up by 1.

- Pressing W will trigger the frenzy mode, where the user can hold the shoot key to burst the enemies, last 5 seconds.

- Gold triangles are turrets, they will keep shooting you as long as they are alive.

- Shooting turrets gives +100 exp and +50 level exp.

- Crates spawn every 30 seconds. After 10 seconds, they despawn.

- Theres only a 10% chance of you getting a +1 to your shots. Failing to hit the 10% chance will spawn a horde of enemies.

- This game is a scale based game, the longer the game becomes, the harder it is to survive.
